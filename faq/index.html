Frequently Asked Questions

How do we get there?

We recommend flying to Vancouver International Airport. Upon arrival, you'll be escorted to our private sea plane. After a 45 minute flight you'll have arrived at our peaceful all-inclusive resort.

How long can we stay with you?

Camp Sunshine offers packages based on a three night stay, a four night stay or a seven night stay.

Are the tents comfortable?

Yes! Modern, relaxed interiors feature king size beds dressed in fine linens, custom furnished living room and dining areas curated by local and international designers, gas heaters, and spacious bathroom with heated floors, and power/charging outlets for your devices. We've anticipated your every desire.

What should we bring?

We recommend you include comfortable, functional clothing for outdoor activities, comfortable walking shoes, sunglasses, sunscreen, hats. Please come prepared for the chance of rain or the brief showers we experience sometimes. You may dress as you please for dining, this is your vacation. There is not a formal dress code. A complimentary laundry service is available upon request.

What's included in the all inclusive fee?

Your accomodation, 3 gourmet meals daily, selected wines, local beers and artisanal spirits, non alcoholic beverages, our unique guided experiences, access to all spa facilities, plus one 60 minute wellness class or spa treatment per visitor.

Can we bring our family dog?

We ask that your pets stay home due to the wildlife and natural setting. Luckily, there are many friendly dogs on site. Who are partners with our guides on several of our experiences.
